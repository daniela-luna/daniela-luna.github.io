My Experience

Hello, there! I'm Daniela, a data analyst with a background in education and administration. As a former high school English teacher, I leveraged data to inform lesson planning, identify student learning gaps, and drive measurable student growth. Later, while managing an office in the finance industry, I saw firsthand how valuable data is in decision-making across different fields.

My curiosity and love for learning—traits that made me a passionate educator—drove me to explore how data could be used more effectively. I wanted to understand not just how to interpret data, but how to manipulate it to uncover deeper insights. This curiosity led me to pursue formal training in SQL, Excel, and Tableau, equipping me with the skills to analyze, interpret, and visualize data effectively.

With experience juggling multiple responsibilities, solving complex problems, and translating insights into action, I bring a unique blend of analytical thinking, communication skills, and adaptability to every project.

I’m excited about turning data into impactful solutions—let’s connect! Feel free to reach me via LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/lunadani/ .
